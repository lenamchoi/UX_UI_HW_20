Tokyo

Nakameguro Street Park

A nice street to walk and view the cherry blossoms. There's also street food along the sides of the river during the cherry blossom festival.

Midori no Sushi

The best sushi I ever had. Every sushi place in Japan is really good, but this place was my favorite. Warning: we waited in line for a long time. Instead of making the mistake we did, make a reservation for your spot in line.
